Your JOURNEY to breakthrough

Undoubtedly 2020 was a tough year for most of us, from the COVID-19 pandemic, economic crises, ..etc. 2021 will hold new challenges for us, like those we’ve faced before, what matters is not the problems themselves, but how we’ll respond to them.

In this Masterclass, we’ll dive deep for 3 hours through a Proven Process for Personal Transformation that will provide you with tools and strategies to shift and transform your mindset and behaviors in order to thrive and face 2021’s challenges more confidently.

You’ll discover how to overcome your self-limiting beliefs and unlock your amazing potential to make things happen in your life for more happiness, fulfillment, and alignment.

WHAT YOU’ll LEARN

  • Recognize that change is possible
  • Enhancing neuroplasticity to rewire the brain
  • Build your empowering beliefs and reshape your identity
  • Befriend your emotions and alter your thoughts
  • Replace old dysfunctional patterns with new functional ones
  • Nurture healthy dynamic relationships
  • Generate your own personalized blueprint

Code of Conduct

Resilience Summit is dedicated to providing a harassment-free event experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or technology choices. We do not tolerate harassment of conference participants in any form. Sexual language and imagery is not appropriate during any segment of the conference, including talks, workshops, Q&As, or on Twitter and other online media.

Harassment includes offensive comments related to gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion, technology choices, deliberate intimidation, stalking, harassing, or recording, sustained disruption of talks or other events, and unwelcome sexual attention. Participants asked to stop any harassing behaviour are expected to comply immediately. Sponsors and speakers are also subject to the anti-harassment policy.

If a participant engages in harassing behavior, the conference organisers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning the offender, or expulsion from the event with no refund.

We value your attendance.
