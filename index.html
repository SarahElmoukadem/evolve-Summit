Your JOURNEY to breakthrough

Undoubtedly 2020 was a tough year for most of us, from the COVID-19 pandemic, economic crises, ..etc. 2021 will hold new challenges for us, like those we’ve faced before, what matters is not the problems themselves, but how we’ll respond to them.

In this Masterclass, we’ll dive deep for 3 hours through a Proven Process for Personal Transformation that will provide you with tools and strategies to shift and transform your mindset and behaviors in order to thrive and face 2021’s challenges more confidently.

You’ll discover how to overcome your self-limiting beliefs and unlock your amazing potential to make things happen in your life for more happiness, fulfillment, and alignment.

WHAT YOU’ll LEARN

  • Recognize that change is possible
  • Enhancing neuroplasticity to rewire the brain
  • Build your empowering beliefs and reshape your identity
  • Befriend your emotions and alter your thoughts
  • Replace old dysfunctional patterns with new functional ones
  • Nurture healthy dynamic relationships
  • Generate your own personalized blueprint

MEET THE EXPERTS

LARA ATALLAH

EXECUTIVE & LEADERSHIP COACH

Egypt View Profile

Lara Atallah is an Executive & Leadership Coach. She is a Certified Professional Coach (CPC) by the International Coach Academy in Australia, a Professional Certified Coach (PCC), and a Group Certified Coach (GCC) accredited by the International Coach Federation (ICF).

An action-oriented coach and facilitator specialized in a diverse range of topics related to leadership development. She has coaching experience for more than half a decade. Lara is the pioneer of coaching retreats in Egypt with more than 900 hours of coaching to individuals and corporates.

Lara has served for three years (2016-2019) as a Member of the Board of Directors of ICF Egypt Chapter – (ICF; the leading accreditation body of coaching in the world) and was the Chair of the Marketing and Branding Committee. During her leadership on the board ICF Egypt was honored with three consecutive Chapter Recognition Awards.

Lara coaches leaders, managers, entrepreneurs and women on how to extend their limits, explore their unlimited potential and overcome their own limitations to excel in both their personal and professional life. Her specializations cover the following fields: Executive, Transformational Leadership, and Life Coaching.

She also completed the "Train of Trainers" Program in affiliation with Columbia University and the Leadership Training of Trainers program offered by THNK School of Creative Leadership - Holland. By obtaining her certification in Deep Coaching Intensive (DCI), Lara is a Certified Transformational Coach. Transformational Coaching is a deep exploration of what one is longing for, and a courageous step into a new relationship with life itself through connecting with one’s inner wisdom. Lara is a licensed practitioner in Hypnosis and Time Line Therapy as well as a Certified NLP Practitioner (Neuro Linguistic Programing) from the International Academy for Training and Consulting – USA. Lara has completed the globally recognized Coaching & Leadership Awareness to Action certification program based on the Enneagram She is also a Certified Thought Coach from Ora Nadrich Institute for Transformational Thinking-USA

Lara is a Hogan Certified Assessor and Certified in the Halin-Prémont Enneagram Indicator (HPEI) assessment of personality types.

In addition to her numerous international coaching certifications in coaching, leadership and development, Lara is a graduate of one of Tony Robbins programs; Robbins has coached high-profile personalities such as Bill Clinton, Oprah, Nelson Mandela, just to name a few. She was a host for several talks in national and multinational organizations locally and globally and has many TV & radio Appearances.

As a coach, Lara is known for her empowerment, compassion, understanding and gentle motivation

FADI EL HALABI

Psychotherapist, Counselor for Couples and Adults, Founder of the Relational Ecology Center

LEBANON View Profile

Fadi El Halabi is a psychotherapist and counselor for adults and couples, integrative stress management coach, trainer/facilitator, resilience and design thinking practitioner and a committed disability advocate and human rights activist.

For the last eighteen years, Fadi has been counseling and training individuals, couples and groups and has intervened across diverse audiences - mediators, civic society activists, social workers, and private, academic and public sector audiences.

His areas of expertise include relational communication, self-care, resilience, wellness and inclusion of people with disabilities. He states that well-being starts with taking personal responsibility for ones’ life and fulfillment; the golden rule being: “The quality of our lives depends on the quality of our relationships.” His professional path in self-care and resilience culminated when he founded the “Relational Ecology Center” (REC) - first within the Antonine University (UA) in Lebanon and later as an autonomous social enterprise.

The REC positions itself as an innovative ecosystem targeting educational institutions and organizations, at large. Its mission is to co-create embracing cultures that promote personal, interpersonal and community growth and fulfillment. Along his professional path, Fadi’s personal journey connected him with and increased his own resilience resources; thus, helping him achieve meaningful personal and career goals.

Helena Sayegh

Counselor/Consultant

JORDAN View Profile

Helena’s Life Mission: “To live a life of surrender and love through compassions and service, to help people find their balance and spread the word of goodness.”

Helena Sayegh is a counselor and a trainer with over 28 years of experience in Jordan, Dubai, Kuwait, Egypt, Syria and Palestine. She is a consultant in areas of conscious parenting, anxiety disorders, managing during time of crisis, mindfulness, happiness, neuroplasticity, classroom management, bully-free schooling and high school stress management.

Helena Sayegh works with numerous schools, organizations, corporations, municipalities, banks and institutions. Helena Sayegh earned her BSc. in Social Welfare in 1989 from the University of Wisconsin, USA, and is constantly receiving training in the latest scientific breakthroughs in her field of work.
